Advanced Features

Powerful tools to elevate your sneaker business

OCR Receipt Scanner
Take a photo of your receipt and automatically extract purchase details including sneaker name, price, size, and date. Save time on manual data entry and ensure accurate records.
Tax Report Generator
Generate comprehensive tax reports for your sneaker business. Export data in different formats for your accountant and get ready for tax season with minimal effort.
AI Price Suggestions
Get AI-powered price suggestions based on real-time market data from StockX, GOAT, and eBay. Optimize your pricing for maximum profit or quick sales.
Bulk Import
Import your existing inventory data from CSV files or spreadsheets. Map your columns and validate data to ensure a smooth transition into the app.
External Tool Integrations
Connect your inventory with Google Sheets and Airtable for advanced management, custom reporting, and powerful automations.
